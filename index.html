    Welcome to My World Hello, my name is Maketh Poul. I am a passionate young developer with a deep love for football and technology. I enjoy spending my time learning about coding, software development, and creating digital ideas that can make life easier and more creative. Living in Kakuma Refugee Camp has taught me the importance of resilience, determination, and believing in dreams despite challenges. Every day motivates me to work harder and continue growing in the world of technology. Football is one of the things that inspires me the most. I love the teamwork, discipline, energy, and passion that the game brings. Just like football, coding also requires focus, creativity, and dedication. Both passions continue to shape my mindset and push me toward achieving my goals. I believe technology has the power to change lives and create opportunities for people everywhere. My dream is to become a successful software engineer and full stack developer who builds modern applications and meaningful digital solutions for the future. I enjoy learning new skills, exploring innovative ideas, and challenging myself to become better every day. Through consistency and hard work, I aim to inspire others who come from difficult backgrounds to believe in their talents and never stop chasing their ambitions. Thank you for visiting my page and being part of my journey.
