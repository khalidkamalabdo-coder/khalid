Hello, my name is Maketh Poul. I am a passionate young developer with a deep love for creating innovative solutions.

Living in Kakuma Refugee Camp has taught me the importance of resilience, determination, and believing in dreams despite challenges. Every day motivates me to work harder and continue growing in the world of technology.

I enjoy learning new skills, exploring innovative ideas, and challenging myself to become better every day. Through consistency and hard work, I aim to inspire others who come from difficult backgrounds to believe in their talents and never stop chasing their ambitions.
